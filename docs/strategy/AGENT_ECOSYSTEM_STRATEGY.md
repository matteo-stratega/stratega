**Transformation Goal:**
- From: Manual execution, siloed agents, reactive operations
- To: Coordinated autonomous agents, proactive content/campaigns, systematic revenue generation

### Agent Coordination Rules

**1. Strategic Alignment (Weekly)**
- Growth Orchestrator sets weekly priorities
- Content Strategist confirms editorial capacity
- Revenue Optimizer validates MRR contribution

**2. Execution Handoffs (Daily)**
- Marketer receives brief from Content Strategist
- SEO Writer receives topic cluster from Marketer
- Email Sequencer receives content from both

**3. Quality Gates**
- All content passes through Quality Controller (voice check)
- All campaigns reviewed against ICP scoring
- All experiments documented by Growth Hacker

**4. Feedback Integration**
- Data Scientist reports conversion by segment weekly
- ICP scoring weights updated based on results
- Content Strategist adjusts topics based on engagement

## Part 6: Risk Mitigation

### Known Risks

**1. Voice Authenticity**
- Risk: AI content sounds generic
- Mitigation: Extract voice profile from Lead Gen Course, mandatory Quality Controller review

**2. Over-Automation**
- Risk: Lose personal touch that drove 60% reply rate
- Mitigation: Marketer drafts, Matteo personalizes top tier prospects

**3. Agent Confusion**
- Risk: Overlapping responsibilities, no clear ownership
- Mitigation: ARCHITECTURE.md defines clear boundaries and handoffs

**4. Data Freshness**
- Risk: ICP scores become stale
- Mitigation: Data Scientist runs weekly updates, engagement signals refresh

### Contingency Plans

**If MRR target missed at 30 days:**
- Shift from content to direct sales (productized audits)
- Increase personal outreach to top 50 scored contacts
- Launch emergency template sale on Gumroad

**If content quality drops:**
- Reduce volume, increase editorial review
- Matteo writes 50% of content, agents repurpose
- Pause automation, return to manual with templates
