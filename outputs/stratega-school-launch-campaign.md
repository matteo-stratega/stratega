## Part 9: Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Response Rate (<10%)

**Trigger:** Less than 5 responses after 50 DMs

**Mitigation:**
- Test alternative opening message
- Shift to warmer contacts (higher engagement score)
- Add phone/video angle for top 10

### Risk 2: Responses But No Calls

**Trigger:** 8+ responses, <3 calls booked

**Mitigation:**
- Simplify call booking (Calendly link in DM)
- Offer async option (Loom video intro)
- Lower commitment (15 min instead of 30)

### Risk 3: Calls But No Conversions

**Trigger:** 5+ calls, <2 conversions

**Mitigation:**
- Review call script
- Add urgency (limited spots, price increase)
- Offer trial (first week free)

### Risk 4: Content Not Engaging

**Trigger:** <3% engagement on posts

**Mitigation:**
- Test different formats (carousel vs text)
- Adjust posting time (8am vs 12pm)
- More provocative hooks
