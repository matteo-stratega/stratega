## Key Insights Summary

### HIGH Threats

1. **BoltInsight** - Best organic SEO (2.4K/mo), similar positioning
   - **Risk**: If they improve their non-India traffic, they dominate search
   - **Action**: Beat them on content quality, not volume

2. **Outset.ai** - Highest domain authority (39), funded
   - **Risk**: Enterprise sales motion + VC backing = aggressive expansion
   - **Action**: Win SMB/mid-market before they lock enterprise

### MEDIUM Threats

3. **Strella** - Best try-before-buy experience (5-min interview)
   - **Risk**: Low-friction CTA captures leads effectively
   - **Action**: Copy their interactive demo approach

4. **ListenLabs** - Clever lead magnet (AI Personality Test)
   - **Risk**: Viral potential for personality test
   - **Action**: Create equivalent engagement tool

### Strategic Opportunities

1. **Voice Interviews**: Chatty's unique strength - no competitor emphasizes voice
2. **Fashion/FMCG Vertical**: No competitor owns this vertical explicitly
3. **48h Turnaround**: Speed claim is differentiated
4. **Verbatim Quotes**: Unique feature to emphasize
