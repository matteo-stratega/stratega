## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Market Landscape Overview

The AI-moderated qualitative research market is experiencing explosive growth, with $120B+ in traditional research spending migrating to AI-native platforms. Six primary competitors have emerged across three threat tiers, each backed by venture capital and targeting enterprise research budgets with speed, scale, and cost reduction claims of 75-95%.

**Market Maturity:** Early stage (most platforms founded 2022-2024)
**Funding Environment:** Active - $100M+ deployed across competitors in last 18 months
**Differentiation:** Converging on AI interviews, diverging on modality (text/voice/video), workflow scope, and vertical specialization

### Top 3 Strategic Priorities for Chatty Insights

**1. URGENT: Close SEO/Content Gap (Existential Threat)**
- Chatty's DR 2 vs competitor average DR 30 (15x gap)
- Zero organic visibility = invisible to buyers doing research
- BoltInsight captures 2,400 monthly searches while Chatty gets ~0
- **Action:** Launch 20+ blog posts, 5+ solution pages, case study hub by Q1 2026

**2. OWN Voice-Native Positioning (Unique Differentiation)**
- No competitor emphasizes voice interviews as core modality
- WhatsApp-style voice notes resonate with European consumers
- Automated verbatim quotes are unique to Chatty
- **Action:** Build entire brand narrative around "Voice reveals truth that text hides"

**3. Capture Mid-Market Before Enterprise Competitors Descend**
- 4 of 6 competitors use enterprise sales model (opaque pricing, demos required)
- SMB and mid-market ($1M-$50M revenue companies) are underserved
- Chatty's $1,100/project is accessible vs $45K+ enterprise platforms
- **Action:** Transparent pricing, self-serve trial, PLG motion by Q2 2026

### Key Competitive Risks

**Immediate (0-6 months):**
1. **BoltInsight SEO Dominance:** If they improve traffic quality from India to EU/US, they own search
2. **Outset Enterprise Lock-In:** VC-backed platform capturing Fortune 500 accounts with multi-year contracts
3. **Feature Commoditization:** As OpenAI/Anthropic improve, "AI moderation" becomes table stakes

**Medium-term (6-12 months):**
1. **Strella PLG Velocity:** Low-friction interactive demo ($150/mo transparent pricing) enables viral growth
2. **ListenLabs Funding Event:** If they raise Series A ($5M+), aggressive expansion threatens all segments
3. **Google/Microsoft Entry:** If BigTech adds "consumer research" to Gemini/ChatGPT, entire category disrupted

**Strategic (12-24 months):**
1. **Vertical Platform Emergence:** Specialized tools for Fashion AI research, FMCG concept testing could fragment market
2. **Agency Consolidation:** Traditional research agencies acquiring AI platforms (e.g., Ipsos buying competitor)
3. **Panel Network Effects:** Competitors with 8M+ panels (vs Chatty's 4M) create recruitment moat

### Top 3 Killer Questions to Ask Prospects

**Question 1: "How many research projects do you run per year?"**

**Why it works:**
- If 1-3 projects/year → Chatty's per-project pricing beats Strella's subscription ($1,100 vs $1,800-$4,800/year)
- If 6+ projects/year → Opportunity to sell annual package with volume discount

**Follow-up:**
- "Let's calculate: Strella at $400/month is $4,800/year for 480 interviews. We're $1,100 per 100 = $5,280 for 480 interviews, but you only pay when you need them."

---

**Question 2: "What's your typical timeline from brief to decision-ready insights?"**

**Why it works:**
- Exposes pain point: traditional research = 2-3 weeks minimum
- BoltInsight/Outset/competitors claim "fast" but don't guarantee 48 hours
- Sets up Chatty's speed advantage

**Follow-up:**
- "We guarantee 48 hours. That means Monday brief = Wednesday presentation. Can your current provider do that?"

---

**Question 3: "Do you primarily research European consumers or global?"**

**Why it works:**
- If European → Chatty's panel and expertise is differentiated vs US-centric Outset, India-heavy BoltInsight
- If global → Chatty's 50+ languages, 4M+ panel still competitive

**Follow-up:**
- "Our panel and team expertise is European consumer behavior - FMCG, Fashion, Food. We understand GDPR, local languages, cultural nuances that US platforms miss."

---

## 8. RISKS & BLIND SPOTS

### What We Don't Know

**Competitor Financials (High Priority Gap):**
- ❌ Revenue/ARR for any competitor
- ❌ Customer counts (only "100+ brands" generic claims)
- ❌ Churn rates (critical for SaaS viability)
- ❌ CAC/LTV ratios (unit economics health)
- ❌ Burn rates (runway estimates are speculative)

**Why it matters:** Can't assess true competitive threat without knowing if competitors are profitable or burning unsustainably.

**Recommendation:**
- Monitor Glassdoor reviews for employee sentiment on company health
- Track job posting velocity (aggressive hiring = funding/growth, hiring freeze = trouble)
- Watch for "down rounds" or layoff announcements (weakness signals)

---

**Product Roadmaps (Medium Priority Gap):**
- ❌ What features are competitors building next?
- ❌ Which platforms are investing in voice (direct threat to Chatty's differentiation)?
- ❌ Who's building multimodal AI (Conveo currently alone, but for how long)?

**Why it matters:** If BoltInsight adds voice interviews or Outset adds FMCG vertical specialization, Chatty's differentiation erodes.

**Recommendation:**
- Set Google Alerts for competitor product announcements
- Monitor competitor job postings for engineering roles (e.g., "Voice AI Engineer" = threat signal)
- Join competitor email lists to see product updates firsthand

---

**Customer Overlap (High Priority Gap):**
- ❌ Which Chatty clients are also evaluating BoltInsight/Outset/Strella?
- ❌ What are common objections prospects raise about Chatty vs competitors?
- ❌ Where is Chatty losing deals and why?

**Why it matters:** Can't refine positioning without win/loss data.

**Recommendation:**
- Implement formal win/loss interview process
- Ask every prospect: "What other platforms are you evaluating?"
- Track lost deals in CRM with reason code (price, features, credibility, speed)

---

**Pricing Intelligence (Medium Priority Gap):**
- ❌ Actual pricing for enterprise tiers at BoltInsight, Outset, Conveo
- ❌ Volume discounts competitors offer
- ❌ Average deal size

**Why it matters:** Chatty's $1,100 per 100 participants may be overpriced or underpriced. Can't optimize without competitive pricing data.

**Recommendation:**
- Run "mystery shopper" demos with competitors to extract pricing
- Ask prospects who evaluated competitors: "What did they quote you?"
- Monitor public RFPs for market research platforms

---

**Technology Stack (Low Priority, But Interesting):**
- ❌ Which LLMs power competitor AI moderators (GPT-4? Claude? Custom?)
- ❌ Fraud detection methodology at Listen Labs (proprietary advantage)
- ❌ Multimodal AI architecture at Conveo (Gemini-powered, but specifics unclear)

**Why it matters:** Knowing their tech stack reveals replicability. If they use off-the-shelf GPT-4, Chatty can match. If they use custom fine-tuned models, harder to replicate.

**Recommendation:**
- Analyze competitor job postings for engineering roles (OpenAI API experience vs custom ML training)
- Review technical blog posts or conference talks by competitor engineers
- Low priority - focus on customer-facing differentiation first
