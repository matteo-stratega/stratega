## Weekly Monitoring Routine

**Every Monday (15 minutes)**:

1. [ ] Google News search: "AI qualitative research"
2. [ ] Google News search: "BoltInsight" OR "Outset AI"
3. [ ] LinkedIn: Check BoltInsight, Outset company pages
4. [ ] Quick scan: Competitor blogs for new posts

---

## Monthly Monitoring Routine

**First Monday of Month (30 minutes)**:

1. [ ] Ahrefs check: All 6 competitor DR and traffic
2. [ ] LinkedIn: All competitor employee counts
3. [ ] Website review: Any new pages or features
4. [ ] Crunchbase: Any new funding entries
5. [ ] Update matrix with changes

## Escalation Protocol

### When to Upgrade Threat Level

| From | To | Trigger |
|------|-----|---------|
| MEDIUM → HIGH | Strella | Series A or Fortune 500 win |
| MEDIUM → HIGH | ListenLabs | Funding or viral moment |
| LOW → MEDIUM | Qualz/Conveo | Any significant funding |

### When to Add New Competitor

Add to matrix if:
- Direct competitor in AI qualitative research
- Has raised >$500K
- Has measurable organic traffic (>100/mo)
- Targets FMCG/Fashion/Food vertical
