## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Strella is a well-funded, fast-growing AI customer research platform that emerged from stealth in October 2024. With $18M in total funding (including a recent $14M Series A), enterprise clients like Amazon and Duolingo, and aggressive growth metrics (4x customer base, 10x revenue growth since launch), Strella represents a formidable competitor in the AI-moderated research space.

**Key Differentiators:**
- AI-moderated interviews with real-time adaptation
- 8M global participant panel
- Mobile screen sharing capability (unique in market)
- Multilingual support (17+ languages)
- Enterprise-grade client roster

**Key Vulnerabilities:**
- Limited mobile testing capabilities (browser-based only)
- Text-heavy insights (limited visual/video components)
- Narrower method breadth vs competitors (primarily interviews + concept tests)
- Higher pricing than some alternatives
- Relatively new platform (only 1+ years in market)
