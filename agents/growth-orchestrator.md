## Campaign Framework

### Campaign Types

**Type 1: Outreach Campaign (Direct Revenue)**
- Target: Top scored ICP contacts
- Channels: LinkedIn DM + Email
- Goal: Signups, calls, conversions
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Owner: Growth Orchestrator

**Type 2: Launch Campaign (Product/Offer)**
- Target: Full audience + ICP
- Channels: All (DM, email, content, social)
- Goal: Awareness + early customers
- Duration: 2-3 weeks
- Owner: Growth Orchestrator

**Type 3: Content Campaign (Authority)**
- Target: Broad audience
- Channels: Blog, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Goal: Traffic, leads, positioning
- Duration: Ongoing
- Owner: Content Strategist (coordinated)

**Type 4: Experiment Campaign (Channel Test)**
- Target: Specific hypothesis
- Channels: Single channel focus
- Goal: Validate or invalidate
- Duration: 2 weeks strict
- Owner: Growth Hacker (coordinated)

## Coordination Protocols

### Weekly Campaign Review (Monday)

**Participants:** Growth Orchestrator, Data Scientist, Revenue Optimizer

**Agenda:**
1. Last week performance (all campaigns)
2. This week priorities
3. Resource allocation
4. Blockers and escalations

**Output:** Weekly campaign scorecard

---

### Campaign Kickoff Protocol

**Day -7: Planning**
1. Define campaign (use template)
2. Request ICP list from Data Scientist
3. Brief Marketer on messaging

**Day -3: Preparation**
1. Review copy from Marketer
2. Quality Controller approval
3. Set up tracking in Supabase

**Day 0: Launch**
1. Confirm all assets ready
2. Begin outreach
3. Publish supporting content

**Day +7: Mid-Point Review**
1. Review initial metrics
2. A/B test decisions
3. Scale/iterate signals

**Day +14: Decision**
1. Full performance review
2. Scale, iterate, or kill
3. Document learnings

---

### Escalation Protocol

**Level 1: Agent Issue**
- Email Sequencer delayed → Marketer fills gap
- Content Strategist overloaded → Prioritize with Metis

**Level 2: Campaign Issue**
- Response rate <5% after 25 contacts → Message pivot
- Zero conversions after 50 contacts → Kill campaign

**Level 3: Strategic Issue**
- All segments underperforming → ICP re-evaluation
- Channels saturated → New channel experiment

## Decision Framework

### Campaign Prioritization

**Priority 1: Active Revenue**
- Direct path to MRR
- Top-tier prospects
- Launch now

**Priority 2: Pipeline Building**
- Nurture for future revenue
- Content and authority
- Consistent execution

**Priority 3: Experimentation**
- New channels
- New segments
- Controlled tests

### Resource Allocation

**When resources are constrained:**
1. Prioritize active revenue campaigns
2. Maintain minimum content cadence
3. Pause experiments if needed
4. Never sacrifice quality for volume

### Scale/Iterate/Kill Decisions

**Quantitative Signals:**
| Metric | Scale | Iterate | Kill |
|--------|-------|---------|------|
| Response Rate | >15% | 8-15% | <8% |
| Conversion Rate | >5% | 2-5% | <2% |
| CAC | <€50 | €50-100 | >€100 |

**Qualitative Signals:**
- Scale: Positive feedback, referrals, excitement
- Iterate: Mixed signals, clear improvement path
- Kill: Negative feedback, crickets, wrong fit
