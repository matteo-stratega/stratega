## Content Framework: Painkillers vs Vitamins

### Painkillers (60% of Output)

**Definition:** Content that solves immediate, urgent problems.

**Characteristics:**
- Addresses specific pain points
- Provides actionable solutions
- Drives faster conversion
- Higher commercial intent

**Examples:**
- "How to fix your lead gen when no one replies"
- Case study: "30% pipeline increase in 6 months"
- Template: "5 LinkedIn DM scripts that get 15% replies"
- Checklist: "10-point ICP validation framework"

**When to Use:**
- Campaign launches
- Bottom-of-funnel content
- Conversion-focused pieces
- Product/service promotion

---

### Vitamins (40% of Output)

**Definition:** Content that educates and builds trust over time.

**Characteristics:**
- Educational and informative
- Establishes thought leadership
- Builds long-term audience
- Lower commercial pressure

**Examples:**
- "The future of B2B sales: 2025 trends"
- Framework explainer: "Jobs to Be Done in practice"
- Industry analysis: "What YC companies do differently"
- Methodology deep-dive: "Why most ICPs fail"

**When to Use:**
- Awareness-stage audience
- Brand building
- Content for SEO (informational keywords)
- Social media engagement

## Decision Framework

### When Planning Content Mix

**1. What campaigns are active?**
- Align 80% of content to support campaigns
- 20% for evergreen/brand building

**2. What stage is the audience?**
- Awareness: More vitamins, education
- Consideration: Mix, case studies
- Decision: More painkillers, conversion content

**3. What's performing?**
- Double down on high-engagement topics
- Retire low-performing themes
- Test new angles quarterly

**4. What gaps exist?**
- Competitor content we don't have
- ICP questions unanswered
- Keywords with opportunity

### When Prioritizing Topics

**Priority 1 (This Week):**
- Supports active campaign
- High search volume keyword
- Direct revenue connection

**Priority 2 (Next 2 Weeks):**
- Builds topic cluster
- Supports upcoming campaign
- Moderate search volume

**Priority 3 (This Month):**
- Authority building
- Long-term SEO play
- Experimental angles

**Defer (Later):**
- No campaign connection
- Low business impact
- Resource-intensive
