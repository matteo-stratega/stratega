# ICP - Ideal Customer Profile

## Firmographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Industry | B2B SaaS |
| Company Size | 50-500 employees |
| Revenue | $5M - $50M ARR |
| Stage | Series A - Series C |
| Geography | US, UK, DACH |

## Decision Maker
- **Title:** Head of Growth, VP Marketing, CMO
- **Reports to:** CEO / COO
- **Budget authority:** $50K - $500K/year

## Pain Points
1. Scaling acquisition without burning cash
2. Building repeatable growth processes
3. Hiring and training growth teams
4. Measuring ROI on growth experiments

## Buying Triggers
- Just raised funding
- New growth hire (looking for training)
- Failed campaign (need strategy)
- Competitor pressure
