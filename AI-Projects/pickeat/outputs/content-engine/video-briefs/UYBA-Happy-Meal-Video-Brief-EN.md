## Creative Objective

Promote the **UYBA Happy Meal** on PickEat by emphasizing:
1. **Speed**: Order in ~12 seconds (4 taps)
2. **Convenience**: No queues, direct to seat/pickup
3. **Surprise element**: 3 mystery gadgets revealed at end

**Key Message**: "Ace of speed: order in seconds, no lines. Surprise gadget included."

**Call to Action**: "Order on PickEat • bar.pickeat.it/uyba"

---

## Video Concept: "Ace in 12 Seconds"

### The Rhythm
A **UYBA volleyball player's serve = ordering on the app**

**Visual sync**: 4 ball bounces = 4 app taps = 12 seconds

### Story Flow (15-20s)

1. **Hook (0-1s)**: Timer on screen "00:00 → 00:12" + UYBA player serves the ball
2. **Sync moment (1-9s)**: 4 ball bounces synced with 4 app taps (Combo → Slot → Seat → Confirm)
3. **Payoff (9-14s)**: Order delivered to seat OR pickup at counter ("Ready at the bar")
4. **Reveal (14-18s)**: Graphic animation of 3 gadgets with text "Happy Meal UYBA: surprise gadget"
5. **CTA (18-20s)**: End card with UYBA × PickEat logos + QR/URL

## Creative Direction

### Visual Style
- **Clean, dynamic, sports-forward**
- **Medium contrast**, natural color grading
- **Sharp cuts** synced to rhythm (no heavy whips or slow-mo overkill)
- **On-screen text**: ≤3 words at a time ("Combo" / "Seat" / "Done" / "Ace of speed")

### Audio Design
- **Background**: Rhythmic sports music (upbeat, not aggressive)
- **SFX**: Click sounds synced to app taps + light "whoosh" on timer
- **No voiceover needed** (subtitles carry messaging)
- **Music license**: Royalty-free with perpetual social media use rights

### Typography & Graphics
- **Sans-serif font**, high contrast for readability over footage
- **Text outlines/boxes** where needed (ensure AA accessibility contrast)
- **Motion graphics**: Clean, flat design (not overly animated)
- **Gadget reveal**: Simple icons with micro-confetti effect (playful but not childish)

## Shot List & Beat Sheet

### Detailed Breakdown

**Shot 1 (0-1s): Hook**
- Timer overlay starts "00:00 → 00:12"
- UYBA player serves (from matchday footage: close-up hands/ball)
- Optional: LUT with sports color grade, slight speed ramp for impact

**Shot 2 (1-9s): The 4 Taps**
- **Visual A**: Ball bouncing 4 times (from matchday footage)
- **Visual B**: Phone screen close-up showing app flow (screen recording)
  - Tap 1: Select "Happy Meal Combo"
  - Tap 2: Choose time slot ("Intervallo")
  - Tap 3: Select seat number
  - Tap 4: Confirm order
- **Sync**: Each ball bounce = one tap = one click SFX
- **Text overlay**: Minimal labels appear on each tap

**Shot 3 (9-14s): Payoff**

**Option A** (with live shoot):
- Fan in seat receives red PickEat bag (logo visible)
- OR pickup at counter: tablet shows "Ready at the bar"
- Player gives thumbs up

**Option B** (graphic/animation only - NO live shoot):
- Animated PickEat bag appears on screen with "pulse" effect
- Text: "Delivered to your seat" OR "Ready for pickup"
- Icon-based payoff (seat icon + checkmark, or counter icon)

**Choose whichever works best creatively.** We prefer Option B if it doesn't compromise quality.

**Shot 4 (14-18s): Gadget Reveal**
- Graphic animation: 3 gadget icons appear with light confetti/celebration effect
- Text: "**Happy Meal UYBA: surprise gadget**"
- Subtext (small): "While supplies last – 1 per order – see T&C at QR"

**Shot 5 (18-20s): CTA**
- End card: UYBA × PickEat logos
- QR code + URL: bar.pickeat.it/uyba
- Clean, hold for 2 full seconds
