## LinkedIn Strategy

**Content Model**: 1 blog post → 1 main LinkedIn post + 2-3 micro-posts

**Why LinkedIn?**
- 80% of PickEat's target audience (venue directors, event organizers) are active on LinkedIn
- Longer shelf life than other social (posts resurface in feed for days)
- Direct path to decision-makers (no consumer noise)

**Posting Cadence**:
- **Main posts** (blog-derived): 1x per week, Tuesdays 10:00 AM CET
- **Micro-posts** (quick takes): 2-3x per week, varies
- **Company page** vs. **Matteo's profile**: Dual-post strategy (details below)

**Engagement Strategy**:
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours (builds algorithm favor)
- Tag relevant venues/partners when appropriate (increases reach)
- Encourage team to like/comment (signals engagement to LinkedIn)

## Engagement Protocol

**When someone comments**:
1. **Thank + Answer** (within 2 hours if possible)
   - "Great question! In our experience..."
   - Never generic "Thanks for sharing!" unless followed by substance

2. **Ask Follow-Up Question** (keeps thread alive)
   - "Are you seeing similar challenges at your venue?"
   - "What's worked for you?"

3. **Tag Relevant People** (expands reach)
   - If comment mentions a venue/person, tag them
   - "This makes me think of @VenueName's approach"

**When to DM**:
- If comment indicates clear buying intent ("We're looking into this")
- If someone asks detailed question (move to DM for demo offer)
- If peer shares valuable insight (network, not sell)
