## Key Strategies

**1. Bundle Promotion (Priority 1)**
- Highlight bundles at top of menu
- Staff actively suggest bundles at bar
- Add "Best Value" or "Fan Favorite" badges
- Expected impact: AOV +30-40%, revenue +€50-80

**2. Enhanced Marketing**
- Social posts: 3 hours before (not 1 hour)
- QR codes: increase visibility + Bar Est placement
- Linktree: "Order now, skip the line" with countdown
- Stadium announcements: pre-game, halftime, Q3

**3. Bar Est Optimization**
- Promote Bar Est more visibly
- Add signage directing to both locations
- Ensure both bars stocked with bundle materials
- Monitor order distribution real-time
